About us

EduGo is a student-centered career guidance platform designed to help secondary school students and Form Four leavers make informed decisions about their future. We understand that choosing the right career path after KCSE can be confusing, and EduGo exists to simplify that journey.Our platform connects students to career information, course requirements, institutions, and scholarship opportunities in a clear and practical way.

Stay connected


EduGo uses social media platforms to share accurate career guidance, educational opportunities, scholarships, and student resources. Our goal is to engage, inform, and inspire secondary students and Form Four leavers by providing reliable content that supports informed academic and career decision-making. We encourage positive, respectful interaction and responsible use of our platforms to promote learning, growth, and student empowerment.
