About us

EduGo is a student-centered career guidance platform designed to help secondary school students and Form Four leavers make informed decisions about their future. We understand that choosing the right career path after KCSE can be confusing, and EduGo exists to simplify that journey.Our platform connects students to career information, course requirements, institutions, and scholarship opportunities in a clear and practical way. Through career assessments, guidance resources, and skills development content, EduGo empowers learners to discover their strengths, explore their interests, and confidently plan their next academic or professional steps.At EduGo, we believe every student deserves access to accurate career guidance, regardless of background. Our mission is to support students in building meaningful futures through informed choices, continuous learning, and self-discovery.
